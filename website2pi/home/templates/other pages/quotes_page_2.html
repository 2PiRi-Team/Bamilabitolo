Quotes for you to Reflect on

Quotes are like pills for learners to energize the inner being. Just as technology tools are necessary to get by our daily lives, so are motivational quotes. As a student and a learner, a simple short sentence can go a long way to brighten your day and ignite a spark into your daily activity when digested subconsciously. A quote can bring that ‘Aha moment’ when the words speak to your soul. That’s about how powerful short quotes can be; words that live longer that the lips that utter them. Technology tools become obsolete at some point; but these words live for ages.
